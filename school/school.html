    OUR SCHOOL



    Welcome to Viveka School, where education goes beyond textbooks. Our primary classes feature dedicated Olympiad sections, fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills. From the 6th to the 10th class, we offer specialized IIT coaching, equipping students for competitive exams. In addition to conventional subjects, higher classes receive abacus training, enhancing mathematical aptitude. Our 11th and 12th classes provide a rigorous academic program, preparing students for board examinations and future careers. Viveka boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped classrooms, science laboratories, and an extensive library. Our students consistently shine in Olympiads and various inter-school competitions, showcasing their talent and the effectiveness of our pedagogical approach. Beyond academics, our focus extends to sports and extracurricular activities, promoting a balanced lifestyle. Viveka School is committed to practical learning, ensuring students are prepared for real-world challenges. Join us at Viveka School, where every student is nurtured to excel academically and develop into well-rounded individuals ready to lead with knowledge and innovation.
