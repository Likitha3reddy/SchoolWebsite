PRE-PRIMARY AND PRIMARY


  • Nursery: Introducing children to a structured learning environment


  • LKG (Lower Kindergarten): Building foundational skills and fostering creativity.


  • UKG (Upper Kindergarten): Developing language, numeracy, and social skills.





  • The primary school is from first to fifth classes. There are both state syllabus and olympiad syllabus for the students.


  • State syllabus students follows the prescribed curriculum to ensure a strong educational foundation.


  • For the olympiad students, Encouraging students to participate in competitive exams to enhance their knowledge and skills. Providing a separate campus with air-conditioned classrooms for a comfortable learning environment.


SECONDARY


  • State Syllabus: Continuing with the state curriculum to ensure continuity and progression in learning.


  • IIT Coaching: Offering specialized coaching for students aspiring to pursue engineering at prestigious institutions like the IITs.
    Extra Knowledge-Based Events: Organizing weekly events to
    broaden students' horizons and encourage their interests.




  • Experienced Faculty: Providing highly qualified and experienced
    teachers to facilitate effective learning.
    offering a separate campus to cater to the unique needs of secondary education.




SENIOR-SECONDARY






  • Science Labs: Equipping students with state-of-the-art laboratories for hands-on experiments and scientific exploration.



  • Experienced and Friendly Faculty: Ensuring a supportive and knowledgeable teaching staff to guide students through their senior secondary education.



  • BIPC (NEET and EAPCET): Offering specialized coaching for students aspiring to pursue medical careers through entrance exams like NEET and EAPCET.



  • MPC (EAPCET, IIT JEE Advanced, and Mains): Providing coaching for students aiming to pursue engineering through entrance exams like EAPCET, IIT JEE Advanced, and Mains.
